 Reverse Engineering
ProtoCAD Engineering staff has extensive experience in legal reverse engineering of CAD designs or printed circuit boards. This enables you to replace either lost/damaged filmwork or convert data from other CAD systems to any of our systems.
We can work from hard copies, HPGL files, CAD data from other systems, or even a bare printed circuit board.
 
Deliverables:
• Aperture list
• Ascii Pads file (Auto (Insertion Data)
• Assembly Drawing in a Gerber format
• Dimensioned Drill Drawing in a Gerber format
• Drill Data in an ascii format
• Electronic files by e-mail and/or CD disk
• Gerber files RS274 format (embedded)
