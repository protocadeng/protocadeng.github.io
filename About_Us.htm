 About Us

ProtoCAD Engineering was founded in 1992 as a circuit board design enterprise, dedicated to the design of high quality Digital and RF printed circuit boards.

ProtoCAD Engineering is a leading innovative company whose founding members consist of a Principle Design Engineer and a Professor of Electronics. Having years of experience as prior members of Fortune 500 companies, both founders have an extensive background in circuit design, signal integrity, PC board layout, manufacturing and assembly requirements. Providing a high level of expertise for each project, the ProtoCAD Engineering team frequently exceeds their customer's expectations.

We appreciate the importance of customer service in this ever-changing environment and strive to become a value added and seamless extension to your engineering team. Our experience in large complex, rules driven designs allows us to support the engineers in translating their electrical schematics into a physical board layout that can be fabricated, assembled, and tested.

ProtoCAD is equipped with state of the art PCB design tools, PADs, Hyperlinx, Ecam and a team of engineers ready to face any challenging design.
Our company mission is our work ethic, workmanship and our commitment to assist our clients achieve their goals. If we don't look after our customers, someone else will.
